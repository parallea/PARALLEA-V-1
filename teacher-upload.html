Drop a video file or click to choose .mp4, .mov, .webm, .mkv up to a few GB

What happens next?

  1. Audio is extracted and transcribed locally.
  2. The transcript + your metadata is sent to the LLM.
  3. If this is your first video: a fresh persona prompt + roadmap is generated.
  4. Otherwise: your existing persona is updated and a new roadmap is added.
  5. You'll see status updates here as it progresses.

Tips

  • Long videos work fine. Roadmap parts split it into 3-7 teaching segments.
  • Speak in your natural style — that's what the persona learns.
  • If LLM keys aren't configured, the platform still works in stub mode.
