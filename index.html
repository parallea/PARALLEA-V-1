How it works

One teacher, one evolving persona.

The product starts from the teacher, not the file. Videos become transcript, roadmap, topic context, and teaching style memory behind that teacher's avatar.

Step 01

Teachers upload videos

Lessons are transcribed and analyzed inside the teacher dashboard, with processing status kept visible.

Step 02

The persona evolves

The platform maintains one reusable prompt, style summary, avatar, voice, topics, and roadmap knowledge base per teacher.

Step 03

Students learn interactively

Students choose a teacher avatar, ask what they want to learn, and move through matched roadmap parts with live doubt solving.

For teachers

Upload once. Keep improving.

Your videos are not a public video grid. They become private teaching material that updates your AI persona.

  • Upload lesson videos now; notes and PDFs can fit the same model later.
  • Review and edit the active persona prompt.
  • Control avatar, voice, topics, videos, and generated roadmaps.
  • Let every future video sharpen the same teacher identity.
For students

Pick the teacher first.

Students do not browse videos. They pick a teacher persona and learn through conversation, roadmap parts, and visual explanations.

  • Select a teacher avatar by topic, style, or profession.
  • Ask what you want to learn in plain language.
  • Move part by part and ask doubts anytime.
  • Use Manim-powered visuals when an explanation needs a board.
Why different

Normal videos are passive.

Parallea keeps the useful parts of video processing, but changes the user experience into live tutoring.

InteractiveStudents can pause the flow to ask a doubt before moving to the next roadmap part.
PersonalizedThe same teacher can explain differently depending on the student's topic and question.
Teacher styleAnswers use the teacher persona prompt instead of a generic assistant voice.
VisualWhen a topic needs a diagram or animation, the existing Manim pipeline can still render supporting visuals.
